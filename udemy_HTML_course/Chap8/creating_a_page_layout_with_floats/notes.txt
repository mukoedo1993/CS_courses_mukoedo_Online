In 2020, we no longer use floats for column layouts because a new
technology named Flex Box and CSS has merged. And it's a lot easier to 
understand and use to create column layouts with so big picture in the next
video.


But...
Floats are still the right tool for the job in 2020 for pulling a 
piece of text to the left or right side and letting other content sort 
of wrap and shape itself around that pulled content.
